This Blackjack has customized game rule.

	- Start with no card.
	- Cards with values: J=11, Q=12, K=13, A=1
	- Can see five cards in advance for each round
	- Each round has two decisions: 
		- First, Player actions Hit or Pass : Hit means receiving a card
		- Second, AI actions Hit or Pass : Hit means receiving a card
	- Winner : 
		- who first reach 21 for the card sum (Beyond 21 == Lose)
	- Both pass for a single round :
		- Higher score wins
		- Same score means tie
